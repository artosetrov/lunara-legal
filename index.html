Questions, answered

Before you begin

Is Lunara's AI tarot reading just ChatGPT?

No. Lunara's interpretations are grounded in source texts bundled inside the app — A. E. Waite's The Pictorial Key to the Tarot (1911), Jungian archetype notes and Golden Dawn astrological correspondences. You choose the voice: warm guide, classical scholar, depth psychologist or astrologer.

How does the daily tarot card work?

Your card of the day is drawn once for you and stays the same all day — even on your Home Screen widget. It is interpreted through your zodiac sign and saved into your journal automatically.

Is Lunara free?

Yes — the daily card, single and three-card spreads, horoscope, moon calendar and journal are free. Premium unlocks the Celtic Cross spread, unlimited readings and chat with Lunara.

Can tarot predict the future?

Lunara treats tarot as a mirror for reflection, not a prediction. Readings are designed for self-knowledge and clarity — never as medical, legal or financial advice.

What happens to my data?

Your profile, readings and journal stay on your device. There is no account, no ads and no analytics SDKs. Lunara doesn't sell your data.
